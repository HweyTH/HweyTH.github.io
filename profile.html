About Me

My name is Gia Huy Thai, but you can call me Hwey. I am a Software Engineer and Data Analyst with passion for full-stack development, machine learning, and statistical analysis. This is my personal website where I would share my projects and experiences. Most of the projects are built during many hackathons that I participated, though I couldn't really say that all of them are good.

About this website, I built it using vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I made a challenge for myself to build a website from scratch without using any frameworks or libraries. That is when I decided to use Jekyll to build this website. I am not a fan of Jekyll, but it is a good way to practice the basics of web development. So, the codebase for this website is not very clean and I might rewrite it in the future.

A large portion of this website was inspired by a website I regularly visited, especially during the pandemic. I mirrored the design of my homepage with Youtube's homepage. The thought process was to make a website that is simple, clean, and intuitive. Yet, my initial plan was to built Youtube.com entirely from scratch with just HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, with the videos being populated from my project collection. However, I realized that it would be too much work to build portfolio, for which I would probably rewrite in the future.

Anyways, I hope you find the website interesting. If you have any questions, feel free to click the feedback button at the top right or send me an email at the contact section.

Thank you for visiting my website!
